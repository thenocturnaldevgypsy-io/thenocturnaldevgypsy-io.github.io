GypsyShards Bootstrap Templates

Summary

  • Under: Open Source
  • Coverage: App/Web, UI/UX, SEO, Open Source
  • Project Month & Year: September 2024 - Present
  • Status: Active

Goals and Concerns

  • To participate in open-source projects and improve my skills in Bootstrap development
  • To address the limitations I experienced with popular "link in bio" apps like Linktree and Beacons, particularly their default templates
  • To create a solution tailored to my personal experience and specific needs

Solutions

  • Developed a project consisting of several customizable Bootstrap templates inspired by existing "link in bio" platforms, incorporating practical improvements
  • Ensured the templates are fully responsive and SEO-ready to maximize usability and accessibility
  • Designed the CSS to be easily customizable, allowing users to adapt the templates to their personal branding or design preferences
  • Hosted the project on GitHub to reach a wider audience, gather feedback, and iteratively improve the templates
